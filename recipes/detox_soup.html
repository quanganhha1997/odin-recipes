Instant Pot Vegan Cabbage Detox Soup

Description

This simple vegan cabbage soup is perfect for a detox diet. It's a tasty recipe that's easy to make in your Instant Pot.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups coarsely chopped green cabbage
  • 2 Â½ cups vegetable broth
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 3 medium carrots, chopped
  • 3 stalks celery, chopped
  • 1medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons dried sage

Steps

  1. Combine cabbage, vegetable broth, diced tomatoes, carrots, celery, onion, garlic, vinegar, lemon juice, and sage in a multi-functional pressure cooker (such as Instant Pot).
  2. Close and lock the lid.
  3. Select high pressure according to manufacturer's instructions; set the timer for 15 minutes.
  4. Allow 10 to 15 minutes for pressure to build.
  5. Release pressure using the natural-release method according to manufacturer's instructions, 10 to 40 minutes.
  6. Unlock and remove the lid.
